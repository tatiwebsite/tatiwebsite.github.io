Log Files

Brandallica adheres to standard log file procedures. Essentially, users are tracked whatever they visit a certain website. This is something that every hosting company is involved with, and is an aspect of hosting service analytics. Information gathered by these kinds of log files tends to include IP (Internet protocol) addresses, ISP (Internet service provider) info, browser types, time and date stamps, exit/referring pages, and amount of clicks. GDPR Rights (Data Protection)

Make sure that you are completely knowledgeable about what your rights are, as far as data protection goes. All users have rights, including the ones below:

Accessibility rights – you are completely within your rights to ask for copies of personal data that is stored about you. A nominal fee may be charged for this particular service.

Rectification rights – you can ask us to correct stored information about you that you think is inaccurate. Further, you can also ask us to complete information that you think is missing.

Erasure rights – you can ask us to erase personal data that we have stored about you under specific conditions.

Processing restriction rights – you are well within your rights to ask us to restrict personal data processing based on certain conditions.

Processing objection rights – you are free to contest the way we process personal data of yours based on what the conditions are.

Data portability rights – you may ask us to transfer data we’ve collected about you to you (or another organization) based on certain conditions.
