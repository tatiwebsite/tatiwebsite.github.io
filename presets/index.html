What are presets?

Lightroom Presets are a collection of presets that come with the free Adobe Lightroom app. They are created by experienced and amateur photographers to make editing easier. With their help, you can edit the image with just a couple of clicks.

Photos are not always taken in ideal conditions. Especially when you have to do it in a hurry. The camera can incorrectly determine the balance, darken, or vice versa, brighten some areas very much. You can fix the finished picture using the settings. But not everyone knows how to do this. There are sets of presets for this. They help you make your photos look perfect.

How do I install a preset?

01

First, you need to install the Lightroom application, you can download it in playMarket for Android and in the App Store for IOS

Play market App store
02

After you open the downloaded preset you need to click on the "Share" button in the upper right corner. You will see a window with a selection of applications to which you can send the preset. After you open a window with a choice of program to import the file, you need to find in the list of programs Lightroom.

03

Click on the 3 dots in the upper right corner. In the menu that opens, select "Copy settings". Select the required settings. (we recommend choosing all settings) Open the photo you want to edit and click on the 3 dots in the upper right. Click paste settings. Done, congratulations! 🙂
